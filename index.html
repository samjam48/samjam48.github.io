About Sam

Background

I am Sam Harris. An entrepreneur and podcaster. I launched my first social enterprise, Pedal Power Transport at 20 which I grew for 4 years creating healthy jobs and reducing pollution in the City of Bristol.

I ran the Bristol Universities business accelerator for a year and have since travelled the world and worked in various start ups in psychology, marketing and healthcare.

Current

I run the Growth Mindset Podcast for curious listeners wanting to learn more about the world around them and the world within them.

I’ve interviewed fascinating individuals from billionaires to actual hitmen to find how motivations and decisions influence our outcomes.

Why Apply?

Education

I am applying to the Founders and Coders Programme because it is the best immersive education in programming available. I enjoy coding and making things work as much as I enjoy working with others and building things together.

Community

Besides the great coding education the biggest attraction to Founders and Coders for me is the community. A group of great students excited to learn together and help each other. I have a number of start up ideas I want to work on after the course and hope to find potential cofounders to work with.

Give Back

I think the F and C scheme is fantastic and I’d love to be a part of it. I strongly believe in empowering others to improve their situation and think help to give back and make it grow.
